100 Days of Lyrics

Aug 16 2016 - Nov 24 2016

Inspired by the 100 days challenge and designer Do-Hee Kim 's #100daysoffonts project, I decided to make a poster everyday for the next 100 days. I will be using a piece of lyrics I hear/think of on the day as text, and two or more colors from www.nipponcolors.com to create the poster.
